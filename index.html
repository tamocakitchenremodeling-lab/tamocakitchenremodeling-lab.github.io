Professional Kitchen Remodeling Process

1. Consultation & Planning

We start by understanding your goals, budget, and design preferences. Measurements are taken, and we discuss layout options, materials, and must-have features.

2. Design & Material Selection

Our team creates a customized kitchen design, including 2D/3D layouts. You choose cabinets, countertops, flooring, appliances, lighting, and finishes.

3. Permits & Scheduling

We handle all required Tacoma permits, inspections, and approvals. A detailed project timeline is finalized.

4. Demolition

We safely remove old cabinets, counters, flooring, appliances, and fixtures while protecting the rest of your home.

5. Plumbing, Electrical & Structural Work

We upgrade or relocate plumbing lines, electrical wiring, lighting, and ventilation. If walls need to be opened or moved, structural work is completed professionally.

6. Drywall & Painting

New drywall goes up, followed by smoothing, priming, and painting to create a clean, fresh foundation.

7. Cabinet & Countertop Installation

Custom cabinets are installed perfectly, followed by precise countertop templating and installation.

8. Flooring & Backsplash

We install durable, stylish flooring and finish the space with a beautiful backsplash that complements your design.

9. Fixtures, Lighting & Appliances

Final installations include lighting, plumbing fixtures, sink, faucet, hardware, and all appliances.

10. Final Walkthrough & Cleanup

We inspect every detail, address touch-ups, clean the entire kitchen, and ensure everything works flawlessly.
