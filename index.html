Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve for kitchen remodeling around Tacoma, WA?

We provide kitchen remodeling services throughout Tacoma, WA and nearby areas including Fife, Lakewood, University Place, Spanaway, Puyallup, Gig Harbor, Ruston, Fircrest, and surrounding Pierce County neighborhoods.

What kind of kitchen remodeling work do you do?

We handle complete kitchen remodeling projects including layout changes, custom cabinets, pantry storage, countertops, kitchen islands, backsplash installation, lighting upgrades, flooring, and modern kitchen renovations.

How much does it cost to remodel a kitchen in Tacoma?

The cost of a kitchen remodel in Tacoma typically ranges from $20,000 to $60,000 depending on kitchen size, material selections, layout changes, and the overall scope of the project.

What is the 30% rule in remodeling?

The 30% rule suggests homeowners should avoid spending more than 30% of their home's current value on remodeling projects to maintain strong resale value and return on investment.

What is a reasonable budget for a kitchen remodel?

A reasonable kitchen remodeling budget is usually between 10% and 15% of your home's value, allowing for quality materials, professional labor, and functional design upgrades.

What is the average cost of a kitchen remodel in Washington state?

Across Washington state, the average kitchen remodel costs between $25,000 and $55,000 depending on the materials used, project complexity, and regional labor costs.

Is $30,000 enough for a kitchen remodel?

Yes, a $30,000 budget is often sufficient for a mid-range kitchen remodel that includes new cabinets, countertops, flooring, lighting, and minor layout improvements.

How long does a typical Tacoma kitchen remodel take?

Most kitchen remodeling projects in Tacoma take between 4 to 8 weeks depending on the scope of work, material availability, and any required structural or plumbing updates.

What is the most expensive part of redoing a kitchen?

Cabinets are usually the most expensive component of a kitchen remodel, often accounting for 30–40% of the total project cost due to materials, design, and installation.

Getting started is easy. Simply call us or request a free consultation. Our licensed kitchen remodelers will discuss your goals, budget, timeline, and create a customized remodeling plan for your home.
